Tic Tac Toe

This basic tic tac toe player just needs to be downloaded, compiled and run. The instructions are on screen, and allows for three modes of play: 1. Computer v Computer 2. Computer v Human 3. Human v Human

The 2nd mode allows for the choice of 1 of 3 different AIs, each with a different personality: 1. Sympathetic 2. Indifferent 3. Cruel and Unusual

Language and Design

Tic Tac Toe is written in C using an ADT (or class-esque) style of design.
